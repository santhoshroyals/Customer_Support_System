# ABC Technologies Pricing Guide

## Overview

ABC Technologies provides cloud-based business management software designed for startups, small businesses, and large enterprises. Customers can choose from three subscription plans based on their business needs.

---

## Basic Plan

Price:

* $19 per month
* $190 per year

Features:

* Up to 5 users
* Cloud storage (20 GB)
* Email support
* Standard dashboard
* Weekly data backup

Recommended for:

* Startups
* Individual professionals
* Small teams

---

## Professional Plan

Price:

* $49 per month
* $490 per year

Features:

* Up to 50 users
* Cloud storage (200 GB)
* Priority email support
* Phone support
* Advanced analytics
* Daily backup
* API access

Recommended for:

* Small and medium businesses

---

## Enterprise Plan

Price:

* Custom pricing

Features:

* Unlimited users
* Unlimited cloud storage
* Dedicated account manager
* 24×7 premium support
* Custom integrations
* Advanced security
* Disaster recovery
* SLA guarantee

Recommended for:

* Large organizations

---

## Subscription Information

Customers can upgrade or downgrade their subscription at any time.

Annual subscriptions receive a discount compared to monthly billing.

Enterprise pricing is provided after consultation with the Sales team.

For pricing-related questions, customers should contact the Sales Support department.
