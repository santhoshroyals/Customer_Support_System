# ABC Technologies Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What services does ABC Technologies provide?

ABC Technologies provides cloud-based business management software for startups, small businesses, and enterprises.

---

## Account Questions

### I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click the **"Forgot Password"** option on the login page.

Enter your registered email address.

A password reset link will be sent to your email.

Follow the instructions to create a new password.

---

### How can I update my profile?

Go to:

Profile → Account Settings → Edit Profile

Update your information and click **Save Changes**.

---

### How do I activate my account?

After registration, an activation email will be sent to your registered email address.

Click the activation link to activate your account.

---

### How do I deactivate my account?

Please contact Account Support.

Account deactivation requests may require verification.

---

## Billing Questions

### How can I download my invoice?

Navigate to:

Billing → Invoices

Select the invoice you want to download.

---

### How do I update my payment method?

Go to:

Billing → Payment Settings

Update your payment information and save the changes.

---

## Technical Questions

### The application is slow.

* Check your internet connection.
* Restart the application.
* Clear browser cache.
* Contact Technical Support if the issue continues.
