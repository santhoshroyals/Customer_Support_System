# ABC Technologies Company Policy

## Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within **30 days** of purchasing an annual subscription.

Refund requests must include:

* Customer ID
* Subscription details
* Reason for the refund

**Refund requests are not approved automatically.**

All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a Human Supervisor before processing.

---

## Subscription Cancellation Policy

Customers may request subscription cancellation at any time.

Cancellation requests require Human Supervisor approval.

---

## Account Closure Policy

Customers requesting permanent account closure must complete identity verification.

Account closure requests require Human Supervisor approval.

---

## Compensation Policy

Requests for service compensation are reviewed individually.

Compensation requests require Human Supervisor approval.

---

## Escalation Policy

Customers requesting escalation to senior management will have their request forwarded to the Human Supervisor.

---
